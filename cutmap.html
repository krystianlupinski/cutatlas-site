01 — The problem

A long project stops being legible.

Cuts multiply. Versions branch. Bins fill with sequences whose names made sense three weeks ago. On a project of any length, the Premiere Pro panel stops telling you where things stand — and you spend real time just reorienting yourself before any actual work begins.

CutMap gives that structure a shape. It shows the project as a visual map you can scan in seconds, instead of a list you have to decode.

02 — What it does

Your project, made readable.

MAP

SYNC

Status tracking

Track the status of every version or sequence — what is approved, in progress, or parked — and read it at a glance.

FAQ

Questions about CutMap.

What is CutMap?

CutMap is an Adobe Premiere Pro plugin that shows your project as a visual map — organizing bins, sequences, and versions into a structure you can read at a glance.

Who is it for?

It is for editors working on projects long enough that the cuts and versions become hard to keep track of inside Premiere Pro's standard panels.

Does it work on Mac and Windows?

CutMap runs as a plugin inside Adobe Premiere Pro 2025 (version 25.5 and later) and 2026.

What happens after the 7-day trial?

After the trial, a single one-time payment unlocks CutMap permanently. There is no subscription.

How many devices can I use it on?

A CutMap license covers two seats — you can use it on two devices.

Will I get future updates?

Yes. The lifetime license includes all future updates to CutMap.

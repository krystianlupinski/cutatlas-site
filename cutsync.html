CutSync by CutAtlas

The edit starts
on set.

CutSync pulls takes from the video assistant as they come in on set, imports them straight into your Adobe Premiere Pro project, and copies the media onto the editor's drive in the background, so the cut can begin while the shoot is still running.

In development · A public waitlist will open later

01 What it will do

From the video assist to the timeline.

Getting footage off set and into an edit-ready state is logistics, not craft — copying cards, naming clips, building a project, waiting for the transfer to finish. CutSync takes over that path, so the material is on the editor's machine and in Premiere before anyone leaves the location.

ON-SET

Straight from the video assist

Pulls takes from the video assistant's system on set as they are recorded — no waiting for a card to be handed over at wrap.

PREMIERE

Imported into your project

Brings the footage directly into an Adobe Premiere Pro project, so the editor opens a session that is already populated, not an empty bin.

BACKGROUND

Copied to the editor's drive

In the background, copies the media from the assistant's computer onto the editor's local drive, so the material is secured and ready the moment editing starts.

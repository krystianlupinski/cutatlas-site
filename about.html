About CutAtlas

A small studio building editorial infrastructure.

CutAtlas makes tools for video post-production, built by people who spent years in post, for the people still there.

01: What we do

Tools for the work between the cut and the deliverable.

CutAtlas builds plugins for Adobe Premiere Pro that take over the repetitive, exacting parts of post-production, the work that is necessary, unforgiving, and a poor use of an editor's attention.

There are two products so far: CutBridge, which automates the handoff of a finished edit to color, online, and sound; and CutMap, which keeps a long project legible while you build it. Each does one job, does it reliably, and stays out of the way otherwise.

02: How we work

What guides the work.

PRECISION

Built to be exact

Editors notice sloppiness. Our tools aim to be precise and predictable, doing one job well rather than many jobs loosely.

HONESTY

No overclaiming

CutBridge is in beta, and we say so plainly. If something is not ready, the product and the site say that too.

RESTRAINT

Quiet by design

A good tool stays out of the way. CutAtlas software fits the pipelines you already use and asks for as little attention as it can.
